Ą and Ę pronunciation rules

  1. Before l, ł or at end of word: ą is [ow], ę is [e]
  2. Before f, w, s, z, sz, ż, rz, ś, ź, ch: ą is [ow], ę is [ew]
  3. Before b, p: ą is [om], ę is [em]
  4. Before k, g, t, d, c, dz, cz: ą is [on], ę is [en]
  5. Before ć, dź: ą is [oń], ę is [eń]
